This comprehensive course introduces the expansive world of AI before diving deep into its most transformative subset: Deep Learning. We'll explore fundamental concepts, build neural networks, and gain practical skills while examining state-of-the-art topics like large language models and generative AI, all while considering their diverse applications and ethical implications. A foundation in calculus and linear algebra is required, with Python experience being a plus. Join us to unlock the power of AI and deep learning!
